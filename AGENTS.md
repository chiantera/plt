## Mission

Design and validate a mobile-first legal triage product for criminal-defense lawyers.

Core thesis:

> Turn legal chaos into a clean case file.

## Non-negotiables

- Do not frame the product as an “AI lawyer.”
- Keep the lawyer in control.
- Source-link claims to documents/transcripts.
- Use DeepSeek V4 Flash for most work and DeepSeek V4 Pro for deep legal reasoning/fallback.
- Design for mobile-first capture and review.
- Meter heavy OCR/audio/discovery processing transparently.
- Validate with real lawyers before building too much.
