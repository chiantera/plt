## Problem

Criminal-defense work is messy:

- scanned PDFs;
- client voice notes;
- WhatsApp screenshots;
- police reports;
- hearings and deadlines;
- audio nobody has time to transcribe;
- facts buried across hundreds of pages.

Most tools expect you to sit at a desk and organize everything manually.

You do not have that kind of day.

## Solution

Pocket Legal Triage works from your phone.

Add documents, audio, notes, and dates. PLT turns them into:

- a chronological case timeline;
- people and evidence maps;
- missing information checklist;
- client consultation brief;
- hearing-day brief;
- appeal/discovery prep packet.

Every important point links back to the source.

## Not an AI lawyer

PLT does not replace legal judgment.

It organizes case material, surfaces issues, drafts working briefs, and keeps sources attached so you can review faster and decide better.
