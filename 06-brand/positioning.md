# Positioning Notes

## Best current positioning

> Mobile-first case triage for criminal-defense lawyers.

## Strong one-liners

- Turn legal chaos into a clean case file.
- From discovery dump to court-ready brief.
- The mobile case brain for criminal defense.
- Scan, transcribe, triage, brief.
- Your case file, finally in order.

## Avoid

- AI lawyer
- automated legal advice
- replace your paralegal
- universal legal assistant
- ChatGPT for lawyers

## Why avoid “AI lawyer”

- invites liability;
- sounds gimmicky;
- triggers distrust;
- frames product against professional identity;
- misses the actual workflow value.

## Better category language

- legal triage workspace;
- criminal-defense case organizer;
- discovery triage tool;
- court-prep assistant;
- source-linked legal workflow tool.
