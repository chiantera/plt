# Validation Plan

## Goal

Validate that criminal-defense lawyers will pay for mobile-first case triage before building a full app.

## Primary hypotheses

1. Lawyers feel acute pain organizing messy case materials.
2. Mobile-first capture/review is genuinely valuable.
3. A source-linked timeline + brief saves meaningful time.
4. Italian criminal-defense lawyers will pay €79–99/mo if the product saves 2+ hours/week.
5. US criminal-defense lawyers will pay more for discovery/audio triage.
6. Transparent processing allowance is acceptable.

## Interview targets

- Italian criminal-defense lawyers.
- Legal assistants/paralegals in criminal firms.
- US criminal-defense lawyers, later.
- Possibly law students/interns who do case summarization.

## Interview script

### Warm-up

- What type of criminal matters do you handle most often?
- How many active cases do you usually juggle?
- Where do case materials arrive from?

### Workflow pain

- Walk me through preparing for a first client consultation.
- Walk me through preparing for a hearing.
- Walk me through organizing an appeal file.
- What takes longer than it should?
- What gets forgotten or buried?
- How often do you work from your phone?

### Document/audio pain

- How many pages do you process in a typical week?
- How often do you deal with audio/video?
- Do you transcribe audio? Who does it?
- What do you do with scans/photos/WhatsApp messages?

### Current tools

- What software do you use now?
- What do you hate about it?
- What do you still do manually?
- Is your current workflow mobile-friendly?

### Show mock output

Show:

- timeline;
- facts known/to verify;
- missing-doc checklist;
- consultation brief;
- court-day brief.

Ask:

- Would this save time?
- What is wrong/missing?
- Would you trust it if every claim linked to a source?
- What output would you need before court?

### Pricing

- If this saved 2 hours/week, what would be fair monthly pricing?
- Would €79/mo feel reasonable?
- Would €99/mo feel reasonable?
- Would included processing allowance + overage be acceptable?
- Do you already pass case processing costs to clients?

## Success criteria

Strong signal if 5+ interviews produce:

- repeated same pain pattern;
- lawyers ask “when can I try it?”;
- willingness to upload real/sanitized case docs;
- willingness to pay €79+/mo;
- interest in mobile capture/review.

Weak signal if:

- pain exists but they will not change tools;
- they only want free generic AI;
- mobile is not used in their actual workflow;
- privacy blocks any cloud processing and local-first is too hard for MVP.

## Prototype artifacts needed

- landing page;
- mock mobile screens;
- fake sample case output;
- pricing page mock;
- 2-minute demo script.
