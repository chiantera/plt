# Open Questions

## Product

- Is the first buyer the lawyer, the legal assistant, or the firm owner?
- Is the first workflow consultation prep, hearing prep, appeal prep, or discovery triage?
- Should the MVP be Italy-only or jurisdiction-neutral with Italian templates?
- Should client-facing intake exist in MVP or wait?
- How much manual editing is acceptable before the output feels useful?

## Market

- Are Italian criminal-defense lawyers willing to pay SaaS subscriptions?
- Do they prefer monthly plan, per-case pack, or annual plan?
- Who controls tech purchasing in small firms?
- How sensitive are they to cloud processing?
- What is the best initial acquisition channel?

## Technical

- Is DeepSeek V4 Flash OCR good enough through the API path Deckard is testing?
- Do we need separate OCR or can V4 multimodal handle enough?
- What is the cheapest reliable STT stack for Italian legal audio?
- How do we preserve source links through OCR/STT/chunking?
- What local/offline capability matters for MVP?

## Legal/safety

- What disclaimer language is needed in Italy?
- What GDPR/security baseline is necessary for early pilots?
- Can we process real case materials during beta?
- Do we need DPA templates from day one?
- What outputs risk being mistaken for legal advice?

## Business

- Is €79–99/mo acceptable for solo lawyers?
- Are case packs natural for large discovery?
- Can processing costs be passed to clients?
- Does BYO DeepSeek API key appeal to power users?
- What usage quota feels generous but safe?
